Our Story

TyreTrends was founded in 2010 with a simple mission: to revolutionize the way people shop for and purchase Tyres. We began as a small family-owned business with a single location in Portland, Oregon, driven by a passion for exceptional customer service and quality products.

Over the years, we've grown into a nationwide retailer with over 50 locations across the United States, but our core values remain the same. We believe in providing our customers with the perfect combination of premium products, expert advice, and transparent pricing.

Our founders, James and Sarah Wheeler, both automotive enthusiasts, recognized that the Tyre buying process was often confusing and frustrating for consumers. They set out to create a more customer-friendly approach, combining traditional service values with modern technology solutions.
